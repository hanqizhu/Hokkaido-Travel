Brief Introduction

It was a long trip from Sapporo to Noboribetsu. And the experience here was very different. Near a crater, this city is known for the hot springs (Japanese: Onsen) Volcanic eruption also caused unique scene in Noboribetsu, which is called "the Hell Valley" by local people. Ghosts and monsters are parts of culture here, which indicates a bless and protect. No matter what its origin is, the most important thing in Noboribetsu must be enjoying the hot springs! I highly recommend the Dai-ichi (which means "the best" in Japanese) Takimotokan Hotel as a choice. The price was slightly higher, but we experienced hot springs with different functions. As well as satisfying service and a solid meal that was much richer than I could ever imagine!. There are not many photos of Noboribetsu, because I spent all day long trying various kinds of hot springs and delicious meals in the hotel!
