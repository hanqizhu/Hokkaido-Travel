Upon Arrival

It was a long trip from Sapporo to Noboribetsu.
Near a crater, this city is known for the hot springs (Japanese: Onsen)

Interesting Norm

Volcanic eruption also caused unique scene in Noboribetsu, which is called "the Hell Valley" by local people.
Ghosts and monsters are parts of culture here, which indicates a bless and protect.

Fantastic Hotel

I highly recommend the Hotel named Dai-ichi (which means "the best" in Japanese) Takimotokan Hotel.
The price was slightly higher, but we enjoyed awesome hot springs with different functions. As well as satisfying service and a solid meal that was much richer than I could ever imagine!
