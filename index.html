About

In January and February 2018, I travelled to Japan with my boyfriend, Keen. We enjoyed beautiful views and tasty food in 4 different cities in Hokkaido
Winter is fabulous there, full of snow, pure and white. I cannot forget that trip, and desire to travel there again. This website is both a memory of my lovely trip, and my recommendations for people who haven't been there. If you are interested, please do not hesitate to go!
